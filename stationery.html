Stationary Stationery

At Stationery Club, a transatlantic Skype interview with the man who invented the Post-it note might be interspersed with pints of lager and a comparison of fibre-tipped pens

This was another collaboration with photographer Emma Todd. Stationery can be aspirational, fetishised - 'buy one to use, and one to admire', and a tool of procrastination. Read Rhodri Marsden's article about author John Ward's book 'Adventures In Stationery'.
